Our story

Built out of frustration.

We spent years watching brilliant engineers waste hours on tooling, process overhead, and coordination fog. Every sprint planning session that turned into a debate about tickets. Every post-mortem that discovered something the team already knew.

In 2022, we decided to stop complaining and start building. Baseline was born from a simple belief: the best teams in the world aren't the ones with the most process — they're the ones with the least unnecessary process.

Today, we serve over 10,000 teams across 60 countries. We're still small, still opinionated, and still obsessed with making software shipping feel good.

Values

What we believe in

01

Clarity over cleverness

Simple, direct solutions beat complex ones. We optimize for understanding, not impressiveness.

02

Shipping is the goal

Done and in users' hands beats perfect and in review. We celebrate velocity alongside quality.

03

Builders first

Every decision we make starts with the engineer at the keyboard. Process serves people, not the other way around.

04

Default to trust

We hire great people and get out of their way. Autonomy and accountability go hand in hand.
